About Me


As a graduate of English Literature, the written language has always held a special place in my heart. The many ways words connect and interact to communicate the myriads of ideas flowing through the mind is truly mind-boggling.

In order to apply some of these ideas, I decided to take up programming and learn a different style of writing--one a computer could understand. After studying the syntax and grammar of Python, I found myself completely enamored with the thought of taking an idea and using code to communicate to the computer, allowing ideas to take real shape and form. I am excited to continue creating applications and projects others can use to navigate this technology rich world.

When I am not nose deep in a book or a hundred lines of code, you might find me playing with my cats or working on a new model kit.
